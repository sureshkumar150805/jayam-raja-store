About Us

Jayam Raja Store has been a trusted name in Tiruppur for years, providing fresh and quality groceries to local families. With 5 branches across the region, we are committed to offering affordable prices, excellent service, and a wide range of products that meet your daily needs.

Our focus is on trust, quality, and long-term relationships with our customers. We source our products carefully to ensure freshness and value for money.
